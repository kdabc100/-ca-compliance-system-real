⚡ How Perpetual Automation Works

🤖 Fully Autonomous Pipeline: Once started, the scheduler runs a background timer every day at your chosen time. It automatically (1) calculates upcoming dues for every client, (2) optionally uses Claude AI to draft personalised emails, (3) sends via EmailJS — no clicking, no manual triggers. The system tracks sent emails per day to avoid duplicates, and cycles perpetually through the compliance calendar.
🔄 Automation Pipeline
Step 1 — Scheduler wakes at set time
Step 2 — Scans all clients for upcoming dues
Step 3 — Checks if reminder already sent today
Step 4 — AI drafts personalised email (if key set)
Step 5 — Sends via EmailJS with 1 sec throttle
Step 6 — Logs result, waits for next trigger
